Centrifuge

5418R

Ultra-compact, 18-place microcentrifuges are quiet and user-friendly The rotor, with unique QuickLock lid, offers fast and secure lid locking with only 1/4 of a turn. The rotor provides an aerosol-tight containment for safe centrifugation of hazardous samples such as viruses, pathogen bacteria, blood samples, or radioactive probes. Rotor comes standard. 14,000 rotations per minute

5804R

The Centrifuge 5804 R is a high speed centrifuge for medium capacity needs. It allows for applications in tubes up to 250 mL and offers additional swing-bucket and fixed-angle rotors as well as deep-well plate capacity for increased versatility.
